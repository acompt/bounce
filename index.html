    This is the final project for Comp23 Game Development. It was developed for iOS using sprite kit on Xcode.

    How to Play:

    The player is given the option between two characters: Tigger from Winnie the Pooh and Elsa from Frozen*. The character starts with an initial velocity and the player must tap the screen to change the velocity in order to collect points and avoid damages.

    Tigger: Begin as Tigger and bound through fields collecting as many honey pots as possible to share with your friend Pooh. But be careful bees do not take kindly to stealing.

    Elsa: Begin as Elsa and enjoy the snowy wonderland making (collecting) as many Olafs as possible. However, you must also watch out for frozen daggers that are not as soft as snow.

    Lets Get Started:

    In order to play you must have a working version of Xcode on your mac. Download and unzip the file (or clone the directory). You can then open the file in Xcode and run using the iOS simulator.
